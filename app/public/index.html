Something Blue is your prestigious wedding planner.

Our wedding is a simple app to help you plan the perfect wedding. Manage your to-do list, budget and vendors all in one place. On the big day you shouldn't just hope your vendors all do what they are supposed to do. If you import vendors from your contacts into the application you can quickly reach them to make sure everything is on track. With so many things that need to be done, we've pre-populated the task list so that nothing is left out. You can add to the task list as well as set reminders for the extra important stuff. We even set due dates based on your wedding date so that your schedule is evenly spaced. Our wedding also helps give you an idea of how your budget should be allocated across the different categories. Get estimates based on your total wedding budget and time estimates relative to your big day.
